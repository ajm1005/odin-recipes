Perfect Prime Rib Recipe

  • 1 (4 pound) prime rib roast
  • .25 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon herbes de Provence
  • kosher salt to taste
  1. Place prime rib roast on a plate and bring to room temperature, 2 to 4 hours
  2. Preheat an oven to 500 degrees F
  3. Combine butter, pepper, and herbes de Provence in a bowl; mis until well blended. Spread butter mixture evenly over entire roast. Season roast generously with kosher salt
  4. Roast the 4-pound roast in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
  5. Turn oven off and, leaving the roast int he oven with the door closed, let the roast sit in the oven for 2 hours
  6. Remove the roast from the oven, slice, and serve.
